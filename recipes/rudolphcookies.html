Rudolph Cookies

About Those Cookies

Tis the season for Rudolph The Reindeer Gingerbread Cookies! These adorable cookies are a must-have for any holiday baker. With their festive colors and playful designs, these cookies will spread holiday cheer and leave your guests with a smile.

Ingredients

For The Cookies

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup of molasses
  • 1/4 cup of butter or margarine
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

For The Royal Icing

  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of meringue powder
  • 1/2 of cup water
  • food coloring

Steps To Follow

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Mix together the flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and ginger in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the molasses, butter or margarine, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually stir the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until a soft dough forms.
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  6. Cut out the cookies using your favorite reindeer cookie cutters.
  7. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Let the cookies cool on a wire rack before decorating. To make the icing, mix together the powdered sugar with meringue powder and water.
  9. Divide the icing into bowls and tint with food coloring as desired.
  10. Pipe the icing onto the cookies using a pastry bag and decorate as desired.
